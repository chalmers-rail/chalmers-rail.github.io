Open Positions

We are always looking for motivated researchers to join RAIL. If you are interested in underactuated robotics, multi-body dynamics, optimal control, or reinforcement learning, please send an email with:

  • Your CV
  • A short description of your research interests (1 paragraph)
  • Relevant publications or projects (if any)

PhD Students

PhD positions are advertised through Chalmers when funding is available. Speculative applications are welcome, especially if you can bring your own funding — we may be able to match strong candidates with upcoming opportunities.

Master's Thesis Students

Chalmers master's students are encouraged to reach out about thesis projects in our ongoing research themes.

Collaborations

We welcome academic and industrial collaboration proposals, particularly around hardware benchmarking, legged robot co-design, humanoid robotics, and cloud robotics.
